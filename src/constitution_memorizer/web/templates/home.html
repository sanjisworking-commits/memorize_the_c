How to use

Each unit is revised through all six recall methods, in order. A revision counts as complete — and the schedule advances — only once every method has been visited; the Done button unlocks on the last one.

  1. 1 Read Read the Bare Act wording twice, verbatim.
  2. 2 Cloze Recall the hidden words; tap a blank to check.
  3. 3 Letters Reconstruct the text from first letters only.
  4. 4 Type Type it from memory — words are checked as you go.
  5. 5 Recite Say it aloud with the text blurred; hold to peek.
  6. 6 Card Flip the card and self-grade your recall.
